Our Mission

Guiding Tibetan students toward bright futures in healthcare

Mission Statement

Tibetan Youth in Medicine (TYM) is dedicated to inspiring, guiding, and supporting Tibetan students as they pursue careers in medicine and healthcare. We provide accessible resources, meaningful mentorship, and opportunities for professional growth that empower students at every stage, from middle school to graduate training, to explore their passions and strengthen their skills. Through connection, collaboration, and culturally grounded support, we aim to nurture the confidence and capacity of Tibetan youth to lead with excellence, compassion, and impact in the healthcare field.

What We Aim To Do

  • Educate Tibetan youth who are interested in healthcare
  • Host talks, workshops, and resource sessions
  • Provide guidance for academics and applications
  • Create connection through online and in-person events
