About

Meet the community shaping the future of Tibetan healthcare

Why We Started This

TYM began with a simple belief: when Tibetan students have access to role models, resources, and a supportive community, they can thrive in every corner of healthcare.

Many of us navigated pre-health pathways without seeing ourselves represented in clinics, classrooms, or research labs. We created TYM to change that—by connecting students to mentors, sharing practical guidance, and celebrating our culture as strength. Our goal is to make the path clearer, the network stronger, and the journey more welcoming for every Tibetan student who wants to serve through medicine and the health sciences.

Did you know?

Our first mentorship pairing happened over a cup of tea at a local café — and it’s still going strong today. Every great program starts with one connection.

Our Roots

What began as kitchen-table conversations among Tibetan students in Minnesota has grown into a community-led effort to uplift the next generation of Tibetan healthcare professionals.

Our Vision

A future where Tibetan students are seen, heard, and represented across medicine, research, and public health — where cultural pride and professional excellence go hand in hand.

Our Board

The TYM board is a volunteer team of students and professionals committed to mentorship, programming, and partnerships that uplift Tibetan youth in healthcare.
