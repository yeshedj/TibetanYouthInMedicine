About

A community dedicated to supporting Tibetans in healthcare

Why We Started This

TYM began with a simple belief: with role models, resources, and community, students can thrive across medicine and healthcare.

Many of us navigated pre-health pathways without seeing ourselves represented in clinics, classrooms, or research labs. We created TYM to change that — by making the path clearer, the network stronger, and the journey more welcoming.

Our Roots

What began as kitchen-table conversations among Tibetan students in Minnesota has grown into a community-led effort to uplift the next generation of Tibetan healthcare professionals.

Our Vision

A future where Tibetan students are seen, heard, and represented in medicine, research, and public health — where cultural pride and professional excellence go hand in hand.
